### 3.1 Know Your Laws
Different jurisdictions have different computer crime laws. It is YOUR responsibility to understand and comply with:
- Brazil: Lei 12.737/2012 (Carolina Dieckmann Law), Marco Civil da Internet
- United States: Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (CFAA), state-specific laws
- European Union: Computer Misuse Directive, GDPR for data protection
- United Kingdom: Computer Misuse Act 1990
- India: Information Technology Act, 2000
- Australia: Cybercrime Act 2001
- Canada: Criminal Code provisions on unauthorized access
